About Us

Our core TEAM

The thalamic nuclei group started as informal weekly zoom meetings between Giulio Pergola (Bari), Shail Segobin (Caen), and Manoj Saranathan (Tucson) to discuss the relative merits of different thalamic segmentation methods. It was then expanded to include a larger group of people, broadly interested in thalamic nuclei. We hope, for more sustained collaborations within the group and across other groups. If you want to join our team and help us grow, please contact us.
